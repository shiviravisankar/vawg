We are Black, minoritised and white women working in the violence against women and girls sector

We convened an anti-racism working group in the VAWG sector in May 2020 to open up space for dialogue, action and the development of anti-racist standards and practices for the sector to start the work to become explicitly anti-racist. The reinvigoration of the global Black Lives Matter movement that summer accelerated the urgency of the work to end racism in VAWG.

Imkaan, the UK-based, Black feminist organisation, were the host for the working group between 2021-23. Membership of the group has been on a voluntary basis, from a range of specialist VAWG organisations, and as such not all founder members currently remain part of the group.
